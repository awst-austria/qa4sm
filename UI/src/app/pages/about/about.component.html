The QA4SM project provides an operational online validation service for soil moisture products. It started in May 2018 and is supported by the Austrian Research Promotion Agency (Österreichische Forschungsförderungsgesellschaft, FFG) in the Austrian Space Application Programme.

From 2021 on, QA4SM is further evolved within the FRM4SM project, supported by the European Space Agency.
