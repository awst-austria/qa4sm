Soil moisture validation service

Validate satellite soil moisture data against in-situ observations and model references.

A unified platform that brings together methodologies and protocols for the validation and quality control of soil moisture data products, delivering traceable validation results you can explore, share, and publish.

Simple

An easy interface for comparing satellite soil moisture data against land surface models and the International Soil Moisture Network.

Traceable

Download results in netCDF and as visualisations, share them, or publish via Zenodo.

Free

Just set up an account to have full access to all functionalities.

Customizable

Upload your own data and apply filtering and scaling options to assess their impact on validation results.

Step-by-step video tutorials

Explore a series of short hands-on tutorials that guide you through every part of the QA4SM platform — from setting up your first validation to interpreting results and publishing them. Learn how to upload custom data, compare satellite data, and make the most of QA4SM’s validation tools.

Project Team and Partners

The QA4SM project provides an operational online validation service for soil moisture products. It started in May 2018 and was supported by the Austrian Research Promotion Agency (Österreichische Forschungsförderungsgesellschaft, FFG) in the Austrian Space Application Programme.
From 2021 on, QA4SM is further evolved within the FRM4SM project, supported by the European Space Agency.
