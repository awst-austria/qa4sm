QA4SM - Quality Assurance Service
for Satellite Soil Moisture Data

What's new

Service for validation of satellite soil moisture products against in-situ and model reference data

that brings together methodologies and protocols used for the validation and quality control of soil moisture data products and provides users with traceable validation results.

{{video.title}}

Simple

An easy-to-use interface for comparing satellite soil moisture data against land surface models and the International Soil Moisture Network

Traceable

You can download your results in netCDF format and as visualisations, share them with others or publish using Zenodo service

Free

Just set up and account to have full access to all functionalities

Customizable

You can upload your own data and use various filtering and scaling options to assess the impact of these on the validation results

Project Team and Partners

The QA4SM project provides an operational online validation service for soil moisture products. It started in May 2018 and was supported by the Austrian Research Promotion Agency (Österreichische Forschungsförderungsgesellschaft, FFG) in the Austrian Space Application Programme.
From 2021 on, QA4SM is further evolved within the FRM4SM project, supported by the European Space Agency.
Within the FRM4SM project, QA4SM has been scientifically developed in cooperation with CESBIO
